About

I'm a Ugandan-American engineer and founder with a deep interest in building products that make powerful technology accessible — not just to developers, but to everyone.

My career spans embedded systems at Bowers & Wilkins (where I shipped real-time audio infrastructure under sub-10-microsecond latency budgets), conversational AI tooling at Microsoft Semantic Machines, and the evaluation and analytics pipeline powering Microsoft Copilot.

Today I'm building a low-to-no-code agentic workflow platform — a multi-tenant LLM-powered system that lets non-technical users design, deploy, and monitor complex AI workflows without writing a single line of code.

Outside of engineering, I'm a long-distance runner chasing my next mile personal record.
